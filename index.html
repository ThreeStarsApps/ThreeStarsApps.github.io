KeyStar

Remap the buttons of your Samsung device.

Description

With KeyStar, you can take total command of your Samsung device's buttons to perform the exact actions you need.

Add up to 10 actions for each button. Not enough? Add contexts to perform a different set of actions based on the currently open app.

Still not enough? Combine KeyStar with Tasker to create your own actions and hotswap configurations on the fly based on your own rules.

PenStar

Remap S Pen Air Actions.

Description

With PenStar, the power of S Pen is in your hands. No longer do you need to depend on developers to implement Air Actions in their apps. You get to customise which S Pen gestures perform which actions in any app.

Whether you're navigating your device, controlling media, or performing your own custom actions, PenStar makes it all possible with just a flick of the S Pen.
